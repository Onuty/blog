I keep game prototype tooling intentionally small. The goal is reducing friction, not adding process.

## What helps most

- Fast project templates
- Reusable input/debug overlays
- Tiny asset pipelines
- Clear experiment logs

## Repeatable loop

- Start with one mechanic.
- Measure what feels good.
- Throw away weak ideas quickly.
- Keep notes for future iterations.

Small systems, clear notes, and frequent playtesting win over complex frameworks every time.
