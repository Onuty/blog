When a project grows, tiny utility layers save a lot of repetition.

## Patterns I reuse

- Date and number formatting helpers
- Strongly typed content accessors
- Narrowed domain types for route params
- Small data mappers near page boundaries

## Rule of thumb

Keep utilities boring, explicit, and easy to delete.
